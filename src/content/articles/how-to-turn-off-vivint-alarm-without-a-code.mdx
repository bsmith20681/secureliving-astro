Are you wondering how to disarm a Vivint alarm without a code? Did you just move into a house with a Vivint alarm system? Are you trying to figure out how to get it to stop beeping at you? Or maybe you want to start using the system to protect your home?

I put this simple guide together to walk you through how to turn Vivint off without an alarm code.

## Use a Common Pin Code or Get the Pin Code from the Previous Owner

In order to turn the alarm off, you'll obviously need to have the pin code. If you were to call Vivint and ask for a pin code, they unfortunately will not give it to you unless you are a customer (more on this later).

In this situation, you can either reach out to the previous owner for the pin code or try to guess what the pin code is. Sounds crazy, but you would be surprised how often people use simple pin codes. Here are 4 common pin codes you can try to turn the alarm off:

- 1111
- 2680
- 0000
- 2203 _(this used to be a common installer code for techs)_

If neither of those pin codes work and you can't get ahold of the previous owner, your options are to either remove the battery or call Vivint to activate the account.

## Call Vivint to Create an Account

Unfortunately, if you call Vivint they are not going to tell you the pin code to turn off the system unless you are a paying customer. The good news is that since you have the system already installed, creating your account and activating the system can all be done over the phone. They do require that you pay for the first month, which starts at $29.99 per month for standard service. The service is month-to-month, so there are no contracts.

Here are the steps to call Vivint and activate the service:

1. Call their panel activations team at 833-606-2573 or visit their website
2. Tell them you want to activate the account and get a pin code
3. The sales rep will take payment over the phone, create your account, and schedule a time to activate the panel (takes about 15–20 minutes)
4. Finally, the rep calls and you activate your service

By the end of activating the service, you'll have a pin code to turn the alarm on and off, as well as access to the app on your phone.

They will not tell you how to disarm the Vivint alarm system without an account. Although, this is actually a good thing — you really don't want random people calling up and finding out how to disable your alarm.

If you subscribe to Vivint's monitoring service, they will create an account for you. Assuming you live on the property where the alarm system is, this will allow you to instantly turn off the alarm. From that point on, you'll be able to turn off the alarm whenever you want. You'll need to pay $29.99 per month for the monitoring service, but not only will you be able to disarm your alarm whenever you want, you'll have your home properly protected. Remember, this price is considerably cheaper than normal since you won't need to buy any of the equipment — you're basically getting protection for your home at a lower price than ever.

Contact Vivint at 833-606-2573 or visit their website to get started.

## Disarm Vivint Without a Code by Removing the Battery from the Panel

If you're really struggling and you've got an alarm blaring in the middle of the night, then you've got one more solution: remove the panel from the wall and remove the battery. To do this, push the panel up and over, then pull the tab out to remove the battery.

It doesn't take too long and it'll turn the alarm off. Just bear in mind that you'll need to come up with a better solution before you replace that battery again — this is just a way to silence the alarm in the immediate moment.

> **Important:** If the alarm is armed and you go to remove the battery, the alarm will be triggered. Once the battery is removed it will turn off.

Hopefully, one of these solutions worked. Remember, the only long-term fix for a forgotten pin code is to contact Vivint and subscribe to their service.
