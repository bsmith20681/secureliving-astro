Although it greatly limits the features, you still can technically use Vivint alarm equipment without paying for the monthly service. Whether you're thinking about canceling your Vivint subscription or you've moved into a home with an existing system and want to see what it can do without the extra cost, this article breaks down everything you need to know.

I'll break down below what can and can't be used. Let's start with the cameras first.

**TL;DR** — Yes, you can use Vivint cameras and equipment without the service, but you won't have access to the app and the system won't be monitored.

## How to Use Vivint Cameras without the Service

You can use the Vivint cameras even without paying for the service. The catch is that they will only record locally to your panel. This means that you will not be able to use the app to watch video in real time or download video clips.

To view what the cameras have recorded, you will need to be at the panel and click on the camera icon. From there you can see what the cameras see in real time and any previously recorded clips by clicking on the "Events" button.

If you want to use the cameras from your phone then you'll need to pay for the service. There isn't a workaround. The good news is that if you have a system installed already, you can use the service month-to-month (no contract) and you'll have a lower cost than if you were to sign up brand new. It costs $29 per month for monitoring with the app and $49 per month for monitoring, camera access on the app, and smart home.
